# Subscribe to Ruleset

This page appears because ruleset subscription links are not enabled in uBlacklist.

To enable this feature, open uBlacklist's options page and enable "Enable ruleset subscription links" in the Subscription section:

![How to enable ruleset subscription links](/img/enable-ruleset-subscription-links.png)

After enabling it, click the subscription link again.

Or, you can manually add this URL to your subscriptions:

<BrowserOnly>
  {() => (
    <CodeBlock>
      {new URL(window.location.href).searchParams.get("url")}
    </CodeBlock>
  )}
</BrowserOnly>
